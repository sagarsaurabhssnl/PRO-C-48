Instructions

    => Scroll over this instruction page before playing this game for first time
    => Keep holdind up arrow in order to make the car accelerated
    => Use up, down, left, right arrows to make car move forward, backward(brake), left and right respectively
    => Press space bar to trigger nitro if available which is represented as a nitro tank at top right
    => The Police Cars are having higher speed than player's normal top speed
    => The traffic cars will slow you down and will decrease your lives if you touch it
    => The tires at top left represents the lives of the player
    => Keep a look at the distance of the policecar mentioned bottom left
    => The nitro tanks will give you nito boost as well as lives
    => Just play and have patience there is a finish line as well
    => If game is not responding then click on the game by your mouse, that will fix it
